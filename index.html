Privacy Policy

TruckFlow (“the App”) is a business dashboard used for managing transportation and fuel records.

The App does not collect, store, track, or share any personal user data or analytics.

Users may upload business operational records such as:

  • Transportation logs
  • Fuel records
  • Logistics documentation

Uploaded records:

  • Are created and uploaded only by the user
  • Are used only for dashboard display and business record management
  • Do not contain personal sensitive data
  • Are not shared with any third parties

The App may request file access permissions only when the user chooses to upload a record. No background data transmission occurs without user action.

For support or questions, please contact the support email provided on the Google Play Store listing.
